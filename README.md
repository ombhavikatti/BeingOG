<!-- ═══════════════════════ WHAT IS BEINGOG ═══════════════════════ -->
<h2 align="center">🌟 What is BeingOG?</h2>
<br />
<div align="center">
<table>
<tr><td>
<br />
<p align="center">
<b>BeingOG</b> is a <b>premium productivity ecosystem</b> — built for engineering students,
competitive-exam aspirants, and placement-focused learners who want more than a to-do app.
<br /><br />
It combines <b>habit tracking</b>, <b>study analytics</b>, <b>real-time social accountability</b>,
<b>AI coaching</b>, and <b>placement readiness</b> into a single, beautiful, gamified platform.
</p>
<br />
</td></tr>
</table>
</div>
<br />
<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">
<h3>🔴 &nbsp; The Problem</h3>
<br />
<p>Students struggle to build consistency. They lack accountability, lose motivation, waste hours, and have no way to measure whether they're actually placement-ready. Existing apps solve <b>one</b> of these problems. None solve <b>all</b> of them.</p>
</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">
<h3>🟢 &nbsp; The Solution</h3>
<br />
<p>BeingOG brings <b>productivity</b>, <b>social accountability</b>, and <b>AI-driven coaching</b> into one polished, gamified experience — so students stop drifting and start compounding.</p>
</td>
</tr>
</table>
